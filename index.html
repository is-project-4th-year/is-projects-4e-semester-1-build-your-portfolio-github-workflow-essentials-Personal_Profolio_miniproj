Trust Academic Credentials Instantly

EduChain is a decentralized platform that empowers academic institutions to issue blockchain-verified certificates while enabling employers and other third parties to verify them instantly. Our mission is to eliminate academic fraud, reduce verification delays, and give individuals full control over their educational credentials using cutting-edge technologies like smart contracts, decentralized identity (DID), and verifiable credentials (VCs).
